Deep learning in the primordial ooze

select a creature for more info

You are veiwing the pre-alpha version of an experement into the co-evolution of neural networks within an ecosystem.

Each creature, represented by a body and eleven antennae, is controled by a neural network running in your browser. a creature may divide and mutate or may die before it gets a chance.

All creatures have been randomly generated opon opening this page and data from the simulation and the creatures, as well as the neural networks are stored on a database.

Many aspects of this experement are under construction. Please get in touch for more information or if you have spotted a bug in the system.
